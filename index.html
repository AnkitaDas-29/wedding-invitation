Not Just an
Ordinary Love Story

It was a typical an evening in Jerusalem, Israel, when Dr. Subhasish, a post-doc researcher from West Bengal, India, decided to take a break from his lab work and browse through a matrimonial site. Meanwhile, in Durgapur, India, Ankita, a digital marketing professional, was reluctantly opening a free account on the same site, only to humor her worried family who were eager to see her settled. As fate would have it, Subhasish stumbled upon Ankita's profile and was immediately drawn to her bright smile and sparkling eyes. He sent her a casual "Hi, hello!" message, not expecting much in return. But to his surprise, Ankita responded, and they started chatting. Their initial conversations were light-hearted and casual, but as they continued to talk, they discovered a deep connection. They shared stories about their families, their passions, and their dreams. Subhasish was captivated by Ankita's confidence and determination, while Ankita admired Subhasish's kind heart and sense of humor. As their friendship blossomed, they realized that they had found something special. They started talking every day, sharing their joys and sorrows, and supporting each other through thick and thin. Months went by, and their connection grew stronger. They decided to meet in person, and Subhasish flew to Kolkata to take Ankita on a romantic date. The sparks flew, and they knew that they were meant to be together. Subhasish and Ankita are getting married, surrounded by their loved ones. They are grateful for that chance encounter on the matrimonial site, which led them to each other. Their love story is a testament to the power of fate and the magic of online connections.
